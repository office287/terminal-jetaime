## Why these changes move the traffic needle

1. **AI answer engines are the new Google.** ChatGPT/Perplexity/Gemini route queries about "macOS terminal commands" through their training and live retrieval. Sites with structured Q&A get cited 3–5× more often than plain HTML. We just shipped 10 Q&As in JSON-LD plus 8 in the rendered DOM.
2. **Google AI Overviews favor FAQPage schema.** It's the format the rich-result generator pulls from first.
3. **`llms.txt` is the new robots.txt for LLMs.** Adoption is small but growing fast, and an early presence costs nothing.
4. **Semantic H2s + anchor sitemap entries** let Google index the page as 12 discrete topics rather than one monolithic page — meaning more long-tail entries in SERPs.
5. **Broader bot allowlist** removes ambiguity for crawlers that default to disallow when an explicit rule isn't present (this is conservative crawler behavior, especially for Applebot-Extended and Meta-ExternalAgent).
